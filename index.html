SINOPIA INN

SINOPIA is an early twentieth century house refurbished with modern interior decor which is set on two acres of lush green gardens, filled with palm trees - making it a stunning hideaway in Portland, Jamaica. Units are perfect for families, couples or large groups (not more than ten) to use as a base to explore the natural parish of Jamaica.


Its panoramic views with an outdoor patio, man-made jerk pit and an indoor lounge that doubles as a meeting place to wile away an afternoon, makes it ideal for hip travellers, family and friends. Guests are encouraged to invite friends and family making this a winning experience. Here you are able to experience tranquil nights and great accommodation alternatives to the resort locations of Montego Bay, Ocho Rios and Negril. It’s not like you’re turning your back on traditional island vacations that include all-inclusive beaches and drinks, but it is a positive alternative experience of the cool countryside, its beaches and local culture.


You can have your meals when you want and also sample our traditional Jamaican breakfast prepared every morning by our chef. There is also a wide range of things to do. Trips to other parishes are available but a trip to a local beach is a must. Portland is famous for its Winifred and Frenchman Cove beaches. A day trek to the Blue Mountains will blow you away with its stunning views and sparkling Falls. And then there is the Blue Lagoon adding that WOW factor with its deep blue waters that change colour, during the day, through every shade of jade and emerald.
