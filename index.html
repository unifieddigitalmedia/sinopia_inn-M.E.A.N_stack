SINOPIA INN

SINOPIA INN is an early twentieth century house refurbished with modern interior decor that also invokes a felling of going back in time. It is set on two acres of lush green gardens, filled with palm trees - making it a stunning hideaway in Portland, Jamaica. Units are perfect for families, couples or large groups (not more than ten) to use as a base to explore the natural parish of Jamaica.





Its panoramic views with an outdoor patio, man-made jerk pit and an indoor lounge that doubles as a meeting place to wile away an afternoon, makes it ideal for hip travellers, family and friends. Guests are encouraged to invite friends and family making this a winning experience. Here you are able to experience tranquil nights and great accommodation alternatives to the resort locations of Montego Bay, Ocho Rios and Negril. It’s not like you’re turning your back on traditional island vacations that include all-inclusive beaches and drinks, but it is a positive alternative experience of the cool countryside, its beaches and local culture.

BREAKFAST

SINOPIA INN is real foodie territory. It is where you'll ﬁnd ﬂavoursome dishes that combine spices and herbs from around the globe. We offer a taste of local food by working with the seasons and using only the freshest produce from local suppliers to create an unfussy breakfast menu with an international fare. In addition, why not Discover the taste of Jamaica, with the help of our cooks. You can learn about traditional Jamaican cuisine and how to source our best ingredients - homemade sauces and juices - to take home with you. Our self-catering jerk pit is also available – perfect for hosting friends and family on those hot summer days.


TRAVEL

To underline the quality travel requirements expected by guests, private car hire services and personal tour guides can be made available. Portland will not disappoint with its many attractions: Reach Falls, Nonsuch Caves, Boston Beach and its jerk centres; and the fishing districts of Long Bay and Manchioneal further along the eastern Portland coastline. In the other direction, we have all the bustling day and night life of Port Antonio with its historic ruins (Folly, Titchfield Great House etc.), local shops, buzzing bars and leisurely/duty free marina. Our city and rustic island excursions take you to the heart of Jamaica where you’ll will find positive vibrations, energy and spirit.
