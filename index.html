SINOPIA INN

Sinopia Inn would like to welcome you to its rustic hideaway. Here you can experience rustic views with lush green hills and skies streaked with colour - not to mention amazing sunsets that will have you feeling the vibes to just chill.

SINOPIA is an early twentieth century house refurbished with modern interior decor which is set on two acres of lush green gardens, filled with palm trees - making it a stunning hideaway. The relaxed environment with rustic views is complemented by personalised services and accommodation.

Its panoramic views with an outdoor patio, man-made jerk pit and an indoor lounge that doubles as a meeting place to wile away an afternoon, makes it ideal for hip travellers, family and friends. Guests are encouraged to invite friends and family making this a winning experience.
