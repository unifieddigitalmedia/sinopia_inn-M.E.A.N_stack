Experiential Trips

Blue Lagoon

By any measure this is one of the most beautiful spots in Jamaica and is within walking distance of Sinopia Inn. The 55m-deep ‘Blue Hole’ (as it is known locally) opens to the sea through a narrow funnel, but is fed by freshwater springs that come in at about a depth of 40m. As a result the water changes colour through every shade of jade and emerald during the day thanks to cold freshwater that blankets the warm mass of seawater lurking below. Local tours are available that will allow you to experience the wonder of this attraction.

Rafting

You can try out the rafting at Blue Lagoon as an introduction to this fun activity. But the original route – the total experience starts on the majestic Rio Grande River from Berridale to Rafters Rest at St Margaret’s Bay – some 12 miles from Sinopia Inn. It is a popular and rewarding trip – a great way appreciate the beauty of the many sides, the many views of Portland and Jamaica beyond the beach.

Somerset Falls & Reich Falls

Reach Falls is one of the many smaller less known water attractions found in Portland that is popular with local people - and becoming better known to visitors. Situated just beyond that sleepy fishing village of Manchioneal in Eastern Portland, Reach Falls is about a mile inland. The clear water gushes over tiered limestone rocks cradling surprising pools. This area is great for swimming and midday fun.
