FINDING SINOPIA INN IS EASY

WHAT IS IT
A country home first built in the early 20th century and rebuilt in the same spirit – nestling on the slopes of Zion Hill, with a contemporary interior decor that complements the parish’s natural rustic charm. Here you can expect tranquil nights and alternative accommodation to the resort locations of Montego Bay, Ocho Rios and Negril. The villa is fully self-catered meaning it’s your home for the weekend. With no disturbances or interruptions as the proprietors do not live onsite.

BEHIND THE SCENES
Set of 2 acres Sinopia Inn was once home to the Beckford’s and is now own by their decedents.


SLEEP
Three of the four beautifully rebuilt bedrooms features ensuite bathrooms with double beds except for the fourth that features two double beds with adding long area and ensuite bathroom.

EAT
Guest can talk through plans with the chef who's up early to prepare a traditional Jamaican breakfast.

WHO GOES THERE
Millennials wanting to enjoy the local festivals parties and events that are hosted throughout the parish all year round ad well as gorgeous thirty to forty something couples holding up for a weekend getaway. Generations renting the entire villa for a family retreat to the cool countryside.


On entry you’ll find a range of spots for solitude. A red-ochre main villa whose vibrancy is brought to life by the vast array of colors set on two acres of lush green gardens, filled with palm trees and variegated plants that make it a stunning hideaway.

Outside you’ll notice the striking floor tiles leading up to the main villa. On your left you have an outdoor lounge/deck for breakfast, wine, cocktails and conversations supported by seasoned hardwood columns with railings - and a doorway that opens into a spacious air -conditioned living / dining area. Adjoining this space, is one of the air conditioned bedrooms filled with both traditional and modern furniture of Caribbean design; wood grained ceramic floor tiles and ceiling work that evoke a sense of centuries past with its wooden beams used to support the roof - sturdy and intact for decades.

If you choose to venture through these gates don't expect to find grand and imposing historic architecture built by tycoons of past eras. Here you'll find the hillside filled with aspiring developments in the local style.

But let’s back track our steps for a minute and find another one of the many spots for solitude and entertaining. The outdoor gazebo, purpose-built for welcoming and entertaining guest outdoors on the grounds, leads up to a custom-made jerk pit. Encased in bamboo stilts and fueled by cedar wood, the design and style respects the island’s culture.

In a more secluded part of the grounds you’ll see another country house where little has changed since it was built. Its facades and windows still remain intact having survived the devastating Hurricane Gilbert of 1988. Today this house gives promise of more developments and an experience that most should come to know and love. This is because Sinopia Inn’s setting creates a relaxed environment with rustic views, complemented by personalised services and accommodation perfect all year. Ideal for weekend breaks and winter getaways. It is an easy escape into the cool hills of the island’s countryside. . It will have you feeling the vibes to just…chill.
