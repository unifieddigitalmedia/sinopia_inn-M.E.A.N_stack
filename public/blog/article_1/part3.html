FINDING SINOPIA INN IS EASY

Jamaica is well served by three modern international airports, and the island enjoys direct connectivity to most major US gateways. Jamaica averages 37 flights daily from the US, ranging from a low of 30 on Tuesdays to a high of 48 on Saturdays. Thomas Cook, Thomsons, British Airways and Virgin Atlantic all fly direct to Montego Bay and Kingston while many other known airlines fly indirect.

On arrival you will feel the sun pelting but also the cool breeze blowing over the Caribbean sea. As your cruise to our country side destination you’ll see a island shaped by a colourful history. Everyone speaks English drives on the left hand side of the road and children turn out immaculate in school uniforms and families don their Sunday best for weekly visit to church .
