FINDING SINOPIA INN IS EASY

Minutes after arriving in Jamaica you could be bowling along the empty coast road , bound for Portland where Sinopia Inn resides. Not only will you experience a cheaper more scenic experience we offer a more local one too. Its not like your turning you back on traditional island vacations that include beaches and all inclusive drinks but it is a good experience of the cool countryside , its beaches and local culture.

Sinopia Inn is perfect all year. Ideal for weekend breaks and winter getaways. It is an easy escape into the cool hills of the islands countryside. Our villa is fully self catered meaning its your home for the weekend or week. With no disturbances or interruptions as the propiertors do not live onsite.


We offer 4 double bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms in all of the bedrooms laid out across one building that can be booked collectively for entertaining a great family / social vacation or individually but with full access to the property – while wifi access allows connection with work and home.
The units balance safety, security and a relaxed environment. There’s plenty of room in the kitchen with fitted units and built in appliances.

You can experience a taste of local food in our breakfast menu as well as an international flare and a self-catering jerk pit perfect for hosting friends and family on those hot summer days. The outdoor deck looks out to the cool hills of Portland and there is 24-hour security including manned gatehouse to ensure privacy and private car hire.


Just imagine waking up and being able to try something new or walking on to your balcony to see a hummingbird or any of our other rare birds native to the parish. It may seem like a dream but you could be staying here soon.

It is extremely homey. Everyone who comes here is like family and is encourage to stay in touch via the website blog or smartphone app.

So come hang out and experience more. Our villa offers access to a wealth of attractions for all ages.
