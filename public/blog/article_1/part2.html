FINDING SINOPIA INN IS EASY

From the contemporary interior decor and units that compliments the parishes natural rustic charm to the higher level of personal service and recognition provided by the proprietors and staff, Sinopia Inn echoes the type of service sought after by so many visitors to the island of Jamaica.

Over the years Sinopia Inn has taken the time to show interest in our guest by going out to trade shows and exhibitions around the globe and listening to what our guest have to say. By doing this we have been able to ensure travel experiences that underline the quality travel requirements expected by guests.


The result a higher level of personalised service that goes hand in hand with the level of customer service provided to our guest when staying at Sinopia Inn. Just ask the chef . Download our smartphone app and connect with our chef who'll be happy plan the type of meal that will help you enjoy your stay a little more or simply choose something from our menu that will have you eating like a local with the taste of our traditional breakfast, fresh fish flavours, classic rice n peas with jerked chicken or pork - and lobster and crab straight from the sea. Thats only if you don't feel like doing it yourself as all our rooms are self catering with food stock available from our local markets whose stock range from fresh , fruits and vegetables only 7 miles away in the parish capital of Port Antonio.

Thats not all , once you have checked in free wifi access allows you to stay in touch while living or re-living your Jamaican experience. Check out the plan a trip feature of our website and smart phone app. Here you’ll be able create an itinerary of places of interest. If you want to eat out , enjoy the nightlife or take part in many of our parishes leisure activities you can be accompanied by a familiar face whether this is your second, third fourth or fifth time staying with us.


But thats not all , even if this is your first time staying with we want to see again and have formed and number or close relationships with regional and local operators to provide you an experience worth remembering. Check out of offers page for more details on our rewards and recognition programmes.

So come experience more with us at Sinopia Inn. Its not all about rum and reggae our destination experience has a lot more to offer.
