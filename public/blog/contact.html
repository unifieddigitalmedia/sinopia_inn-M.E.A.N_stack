QUESTIONS? FEEDBACK? COMPLAINTS?

We always love to hear feedback from our readers, and do read every email and appreciate the time you put into writing to us. Please note that we receive hundreds of emails every week, it actually got to the point where it's nearly impossible to get back at everyone. If we don't reply, don't get hurt. It's not you, it's us (unless you're sending me some creepy pictures, then it's you).

ARE YOU A COMPANY?

If you're interested to work with us and possibly want your brand featured on our blog? That's fantastic, we'd love to hear from you. Head directly over to the WORKING WITH US page and get in touch with us there. Thank you.

ARE YOU A READER?

Awesome, we love you! But before you shoot us an email, please check below if your question can be answered. These are some of the most common emails we get...

I want to become a travel blogger like you. Can you help me?
Yes we can, but this is not something that I can just do within a few minutes. If you have travel blogging related questions and you want some expert advice, you might consider scheduling a Skype session with me and we can talk a full hour ($99/hour).

Can you plan my vacation or give me info about the weather, sightseeing, visas, etc?
Unfortunately I don't have the time to create individual travel itineraries.

Can you promote my blog post / funding campaign / video / infographic / book / etc. for free?
We get emails like this all the time. If we would promote every day all the stuff people wants us to promote, our site would turn into a spam hole. So... No.

Can I send you a guest post with a link back to my website?
If you want to include a link to a company website, please contact me through our work with us page, and we'll send you our rates. Wait... you're a travel blogger? At the moment I do not accept more guest posts from fellow bloggers. Feel free to check back later. I will update this part if anything changes.

Can I interview you / ask you to contribute to a collab post / answer questions in a survey?
We get way too many interview and collaboration requests from fellow bloggers, so I completely cut down on those. However, I might be available for press, news websites, travel magazines and publications. Please send an inquiry through my work with me page.
