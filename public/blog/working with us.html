I arrived on a direct flight into Montego Bay and checked in at a luxurious villa at Round Hill,with Ralph Lauren designed oceanfront rooms. After relaxing on the white sand beach, I tried some local jerk. In addition to sampling spa treatment, I'd make sure I found time to try some of Jamaicas exciting activites, like zip lining through the treetops or riding the bobsled down Mystic Mountain. In the evening i'd head down to the strip for rum and cocktails in the bars and cafes and would be sure to end the night with dance at Margaritaville.
