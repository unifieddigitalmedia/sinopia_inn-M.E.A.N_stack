DISCOVERING JA - A SIX PART SERIES

Returning to my urban dwelling on the outskirts of London felt surreal. I couldn't believe that 7 weeks flew by so quickly, It really only felt like two. It looked like two as well. My plants were still alive and despite the change in weather from luke warm to freezing everything else seem to remain in tact.

Everyone was so shocked when they saw me. Some where even cheeky. They even made jokes that I brought back more than just a little piece of the island sunshine. I laughed because it was true. I gained a lot of weight plus inspiration for new recipes, that Ill be sharing.
