Food And Travel

Jamaica has long been a jewel in the Caribbean. Famously known for its legendary rum, laid back and relaxed atmosphere of the island and its people - but there’s far more to discover. It is one of the few destinations in the world where beaches have remained completely unspoiled. A destination that unlocks the mystery of nature – an oasis with amazing sunsets and oceanic views which are an ideal escape for island lovers. Not to mention being the birthplace of reggae music.


Every stay with Sinopia Inn is like no other, it’s a journey to the heart of island where the true essence lies. Every trip is an opportunity to see a different facet of what makes Jamaica, Jamaica. Simply Day Passes or speak with us when you arrive and we'll be happy to arrange a trip for you.

CULINARY EXPERIENCES

Discover the taste of Jamaica, with the help of our cooks. You can learn about traditional Jamaican cuisine and how to source our best ingredients - homemade sauces and juices - to take home with you.


BEACHES

Walk down the beaches near Port Antonio Jamaica and be flushed with sun, sand, sea and beautiful people enjoying island life. You won’t have a beach on your door step but our shuttle transport means you'll still have access to one.

MOUNTAIN AND BIKE TREKS

The Blue Mountains peak is the highest on the island, rising to an altitude of 7,402 feet. This treks offer a chance to pit your stamina against the most beautiful backdrop in Jamaica and a stunning sunrise view.


YOGA

Mediation, prayer ,yoga and restful contemplation in silence



EXPERIENTIAL TRIPS

Explore Jamaica, its adventurous trails and authentic activities. From the beaches and many sought after tanning spots to rich turquoise colored lagoons, hidden waterfalls and subtle breezes while rafting down the Rio Grande river. You'll be sure to immerse yourself in the Jamaican destination experience.


LOCAL EXPERIENCES

The birth place of Bond, Bolt and Bob Marley naturally offers good food, great music and panoramic scenery. Experience the island like a local and bring back home a ray of sunshine and local culture.



FAMILY AND SPORTING

Family activities are designed to bring every generation together. There is loads you can enjoy as a family: like cheering favourites in our annual weeklong sport-fishing event, sightseeing, bird watching and tracing your ancestry.
