FINDING SINOPIA INN IS EASY

With Sinopia Inn every stay is a path to the heart of Jamaica – to the true essence of the country. This is a country home. It was first built in the early 20th century as a country home and rebuilt in the same spirit – nestling on the slopes of Zion Hill, with a contemporary interior decor that complements the parishe's natural rustic charm.

It is a great holiday location only 7 miles from the parish capital of Port Antonio with the Blue Lagoon (Blue Hole) and Winnifred Beach (Jamaica’s best) within walking distance either way. Close also are the internationally loved beaches of Frenchman’s Cove and San San.

Our services
